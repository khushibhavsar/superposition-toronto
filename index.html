About Us

Our Mission

Women are only 23% of science and technology workers in Canada. We often hear the reasoning, "Girls just aren't into STEM," but let's ask why.

Learn More

Our Impact

In less than a year, we have reached 10,000+ individuals in 6 provinces, 70 countries, and all inhabited continents through our events and social media.

Learn More

Our Initiatives

Our approach is to actively bring girls into STEM by hosting workshops, panels, contests, and hackathons that equip them with both soft and hard skills.

Testimonials
Samantha Ouyang

Founder & Executive Director

For too long, I simply accepted the fact that some things were just going to remain the way they were. When my male friends took jabs at my coding skills, I just laughed with them too - I thought it was just something I was going to have to put up with. No one ever wants to call it out, because we all just want to avoid trouble and pretend that things are fine, when in reality, that's far from the truth. I didn't think anything could really change - but that's because no one was actually trying. So I took a chance and tried. I wanted to create a community of individuals with similar interests/goals, to provide educational opportunities on topics that schools don’t teach, and for girls to pursue their passions with confidence rather than doubt.

Nikita Yang

Senior Director of Content Creation

Joining Superposition Toronto was honestly one of the best decisions I made last year. As the Co-Director of Operations, I’ve gained and cultivated my leadership, time management, organization, and communication skills, and I’ve been able to oversee, work, and network with so many amazing, like-minded individuals! It’s a spectacular journey to see every single one of our events go from the brainstorming stage to planning and finally seeing it actualized. It’s also been a joy to see how many people are interested in our mission to bridge the gender gap in STEM and participate in our events to further their own education outside of school! The culture here at Superposition Toronto is welcoming, inclusive, and supportive as well. We have executive socials every month and everyone’s friends with everyone on the team.
